About Us!

This website was created to develop html, css, and javascript skills for ITIS 3135 Web App Design and Development. The goal of the website is to build these three skills in a unique way. My client for this project is my little brother, Ian. He plays many instruments and this website serves as not only a way to "play" those instruments, but also as an archive. Ian knows how to play too many instruments to count and with this website, maybe you can too.

Most of the guitars on this website have been in the family for decades. My grandfather is a musician who has played guitar for most of his life. He has inspired both my mother and little brother, Ian, to learn how to play guitar and bass. Ian is involved in numerous bands at his school and has performed with some of the instruments you can see on this website. Some of the instruments not shown include: flute, mandolin, saxophone, piano, kalimba, ukulele, and banjo.

I am a junior at UNC Charlotte studying computer informatics. My interests include robotics, artificial intelligence, and bioinformatics. If you have any more questions, feel free to contact us on the contact page here.
